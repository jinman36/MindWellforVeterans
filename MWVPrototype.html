MindWell for Veterans || Prototype

Transitioning from military to civilian life can be challenging for many veterans, and health issues are common among many of them. However, navigating the complex landscape of Veteran Affairs managed health resources can be overwhelming for veterans, leading to underutilization of available support services.

MindWell for Veterans (MWV)

MWV will address these challenges by providing a specialized platform tailored to the unique needs of veterans by tying all these disjointed resources together, offering easy access to health resources and support.

What exactly is the purpose of the App project?

MindWell for Vets is a centralized platform for health resources, providing easy access to all the different services and tools that veterans need.

What are your goals in completing this App project?

  • Create a searchable directory of veteran's resources.
  • Educational articles and videos for navigating the VA and other Veteran Affiliated resources.
  • Create a website with an appointment booking system to connect with someone to help walk through some of the steps.

What is your motivation for working on this App project?

The Veterans Affairs Office and sites are horrible, and they need help.

How is the App project related to other systems?

The app will need to be designed to integrate existing systems such as:

  • Veterans Affairs (VA)
  • Electronic Health Records (EMR)
  • Telehealth Platforms
  • Community-Based Organizations (VFW, VSO)

Why is this App project important or interesting?

Creating a centralized platform that integrates each of these other services will keep the user experience manageable while still improving the care that Veterans receive from the different services.
